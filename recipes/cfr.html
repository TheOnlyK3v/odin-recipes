Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This chicken fried rice is a recipe of my own creation and based off a mix of hibachi style rice and regular chicken fried rice. It's relatively simple and goes great with some yum yum sauce. You can add flavor in many ways but the recipe itself is fairly bare bones and simple to make. This recipe will curb your craving for hibachi style fried rice and make you realize how easy it is to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken
  • 2 Cups Rice
  • 2 Eggs
  • Mixed Peas and Carrots
  • Soy Sauce
  • 4 Tbsp Butter
