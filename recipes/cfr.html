Chicken Fried Rice

Description

This chicken fried rice is a recipe of my own creation and based off a mix of hibachi style rice and regular chicken fried rice. It's relatively simple and goes great with some yum yum sauce. You can add flavor in many ways but the recipe itself is fairly bare bones and simple to make. This recipe will curb your craving for hibachi style fried rice and make you realize how easy it is to make at home.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken
  • 2 Cups Rice
  • 2 Eggs
  • Mixed Peas and Carrots
  • Soy Sauce
  • 4 Tbsp Butter

Steps

  1. Start by ensuring you prepare your rice ahead of time, typically the day before is best for good fried rice but you can make do regardless. Once it's ready go ahead and preheat a skillet to medium-high heat and add your butter.
  2. While that's frying go ahead and start adding soy sauce until you reach your desired color and taste.
  3. Chop and prepare your chicken in your desired fashion, we usually lightly season and pan sear it
  4. While your chicken is cooking go ahead and scramble your eggs then add them into your rice
  5. Next prepare your peas and carrots, we usually use the frozen bag so this generally just involves putting them in the microwave.
  6. Lastly mix it all together and let it warm before serving.
